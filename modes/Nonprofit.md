TYPE: Nonprofit / Advocacy
When you see this signal:
  - Emotional story posts get high shares but low conversions (donations, signatures).

Do this next (action list):
  1) Create a 45–60s "impact" reel: person → problem → solution + exact ask (donate/sign).  
  2) Add a clear micro-ask: "Sign petition — takes 10s" or "Donate ₹50 and we will {impact}".  
  3) Use story link + highlight and thank donors publicly (with consent) to generate social proof.

3 quick variations to A/B test:
  - V1: Micro-ask (signature) vs donation CTA.  
  - V2: Emotional single story vs multiple beneficiary montage.  
  - V3: Short reel vs longer explanation with link to landing page.

Caption / Hook templates:
  Hook (0–3s): "Meet {name}. With ₹50 we can give them {impact}."  
  Caption: "Sign: {link}. Donate: {link}. Even one share helps — tap share."

What to measure & expected win:
  - Track: conversion rate on landing, shares, donation amount.  
  - Expected: small donation uplift if you make the ask specific and low friction (₹50).

48-hour checklist:
  - Post impact reel + pin donation/sign link.  
  - Send an email to current list with the same ask.  
  - Share donor thank-you story.
