TYPE: B2B / SaaS / Product Marketing
When you see this signal:
  - A technical demo post has high reach but low demo signups or trial starts.

Do this next (action list):
  1) Convert the demo into a short "Outcomes" clip: show metric before → after (e.g., "Cut process time 40%").  
  2) Add a one-click demo CTA: "Book 15-min setup call" (link in bio).  
  3) Share a 1-page case study PDF as gated content for email capture.

3 quick variations to A/B test:
  - V1: Outcome-driven CTA (ROI) vs feature CTA (feature list).  
  - V2: Demo clip vs customer testimonial clip.  
  - V3: Gated PDF vs gated video walkthrough.

Caption / Hook templates:
  Hook (0–3s): "How {company} saved 40% time with {product}"  
  Caption: "Want results like this? Book a 15-min demo → link in bio. Case study download available."

What to measure & expected win:
  - Track: demo bookings, trial starts, form fills.  
  - Expected: demo signups ↑ significantly if content leads with ROI and has an easy booking flow.

  - Create short ROI clip + landing page with 15-min booking.
